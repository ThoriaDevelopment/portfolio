02 Falling Blocks Mini-game

Where: The hero background on desktop.

Semi-transparent squares drift down like falling blocks, gently repel from your cursor, and explode into a burst when you click. The animation pauses when the hero scrolls off-screen or the tab is hidden.

How to play: Move the mouse across the hero to push blocks aside, then click anywhere to detonate a particle burst. The longer you hang around, the more blocks fall.

Achievement: Clicking the hero area unlocks Block Breaker.
